What is Jiu Gong Paper?

Jiu Gong Ge Paper is designed to help learners practice complicated Chinese characters.

While tian zi ge paper would suffice for learning very basic Chinese characters, jiu gong ge helps the learner break up complex characters into smaller, manageable sections.

Of course, nine 九 is the best way to describe this grid format.

You can type "jiu" in your preferred pinyin input method editor to easily find this basic number character.

This Chinese character writing template will help learners improve the proportions of characters over extended practice for improved penmanship.

Like the other specialized Chinese character writing practice templates, jiu gong ge is designed to help the learner carefully practice pen or brush strokes.

Hui gong ge paper is a special Chinese writing template with a flexible grid design.

The recurring motif for the variations of the design is that the concenctric rectangles form a donut shape on each grid.

The Chinese word that "looks like" donut is return 回.

You can type "hui" in your preferred pinyin input method editor to easily find the character.

Writing Chinese calligraphy on this writing paper template can be tricky with a traditional brush, so using a pen or brush pen is highly recommended.

If you are learning grass-style calligraphy, the wide area Chinese writing paper template is a good choice. You will be focused on keep your characters within a reasonable size while working on form and quality.

If you are still working on your penmanship, you may want to use the hybrid mi zi hui gong ge writing paper template. The complex array of lines provides the perfect template for formal instruction by a teacher or tutor.

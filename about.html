This project is designed to share printable (and markable) Chinese character writing practice paper and other Chinese learning resources, as freely as possible.

Printing

From Google Chrome, you can print this paper to any cloud printer, like your local FedEx Office Online Printing Service.

Learn more about FedEx Office Cloud Printing options avaiable in your area.

To save you time (and fuel), you can even have FedEx ship your writing practice paper directly to you.

If you need the paper sooner, print to any printer and pick up your paper.

For Teachers: Use this paper for the classroom exercises, homework or academic publishing.

For Parents: Take charge of your children's Chinese language education. Structure home lessons based on skills and values that are important to you.

For Vendors: You have permission to print, bundle and/or sell paper or any works on this site for any price, where allowed by law.
